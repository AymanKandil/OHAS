Diseases Caused By Worms

Add a Disease
# Diseases Pathogen responsible and its habitat Mode of transmission Main symptoms Treatment
1 Hook-worm Ancylostoma duobenale Transmission from person to person and filariform larvae passed out in faeces Dermatitis; reddish, severe anaemia; duodenal ulcer, constipation. Anthelminthic medications
2 Ascariasis Ascaris Lumbricoides Transmission from person to person Larvae in lung cause pneumonia and typhoid-like fever Anthelmintic medications
3 Pinworm Enterobius vermicularis, caecum and vermiform appendix Transmission from one person to another by ingestion of eggs in contaminated food or drink. Eczematous condition round the anus and bed wetting at night mebendazole, pyrantel pamoate, or albendazole
4 Filariasis Wuchereria Bancrofti Mosquito bite larvae deposited on skin Elephantiasis Diethylcarbamazine citrate (DEC)
