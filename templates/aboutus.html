OHAS

With the spread of COVID-19 many things have swapped over to the digital world. Have swapped their main operations to online retail. However, an area that is lacking in this department is the agriculture sector of Kuwait. With the aid of this project, we would be able to provide farmers of Kuwait access to a website that would allow them to check weather patterns to better know upcoming weather. Allow them a place to show their farm and products to be able to better network and find people interested in their farm and their products. We believe that pushing farmers to the digital age would only add benefits to their work.
